Hello There!

Welcome to my personal corner of the digital world. I am Anish Prasad Lohani, a high school graduate whose academic journey has been marked by excellence and dedication. As the valedictorian of my high school, I achieved the coveted Rank 1 and secured the gold medal in the Nepal Chemistry Olympiad, demonstrating my unwavering commitment to the sciences. Additionally, my accomplishments extend to the national stage, where I earned the Rank 4 position in the Nepal Biology Olympiad and a commendable Rank 5 in the Nepal Junior Science Olympiad.

Beyond academics, my heart lies in community service. I have collaborated closely with the Autism Society, empowering autistic children with essential skills in reading, writing, speaking, and arithmetic. Simultaneously, I have educated their parents and teachers on effective approaches to combat against Autism. Working with the Lions Club, I have contributed to sustainable tree plantation initiatives, preserving the environment. Furthermore, my partnership with the Leprosy Foundation involved groundbreaking research on antibiotic resistance in leprosy patients and the development of ayurvedic remedies.

Looking ahead, I envision a future where I major in Chemistry and Applied Chemistry, returning to Nepal to establish a chemical fertilizer industry. My goal is to provide affordable, high-quality fertilizers to Nepali farmers, thereby bolstering the nation's GDP and fostering the growth of medicinal herbs for Nobel medicine production. Together, we can sow the seeds of progress and elevate Nepal to new heights.
